Our history

Bookra bank started its services in 1999, this was after hamood's cousin who had recently moved to the UAE from the United kingdom was unable to obtain a morgage from any of the banks due to being a high risk customer. therefore, with the help of some friends, hamood had opened a small bank in the UAE which had enabled him to have the facility to provide morgage's to his cousin and many other people in the same situation.
